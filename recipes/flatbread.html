Flatbread

Description

Easy high-protein flatbread recipe. As a side to dip in soups or stews, or use as a wrap for an easy meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g self rising flour
  • 200g Greek yogurt
  • .5t salt
  • 3T fresh herbs of choice

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients in a large bowl until a soft dough forms.
  2. Cover bowl with a towel and let the dough rest for 30 minutes.
  3. After the dough has rested, portion out into 3 balls. Roll out each dough ball until about 1/4 inch thick
  4. Heat a frying pan with medium heat. Add a small amount of oil.
  5. When the oil is hot, add the rolled out flatbread and cook on each side until goden brown and a little puffed up.
  6. Remove from heat and enjoy!
